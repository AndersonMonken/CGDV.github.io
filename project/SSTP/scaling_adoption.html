Key Findings

How Farmers Acquire Seeds

Ghana

61.9% of farmers purchased maize at full cost

Mozambique

95% got cassava for free

Tanzania
  • 90% purchased maize at full price
  • 86% purchased beans at full price
  • 67% purchased at full price
Farmers most likely to purchase improved seeds if source is within reasonable distance
  • Ghana: 6.85km reduced
  • Tanzania: 0.31km reduced
Barriers to Adoption
  • Affordability – 44% direct beneficiaries in Ghana
  • Don’t know where to get inputs – 53% indirect beneficiaries Mozambique
  • Lack of knowledge by indirect beneficiaries
  • Lack of interest in trying something new
  • Adoption most likely among male farmers than female farmers in Ghana and Mozambique
  • Less educated less likely to adopt
