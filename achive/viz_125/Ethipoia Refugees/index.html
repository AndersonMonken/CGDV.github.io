Ethiopia: Safe Harbour for Refugees in Africa

UNHCR staff presence on field in Ethiopia

Ethiopia has a long history of hosting refugees and maintains an open-door asylum policy, giving humanitarian access and protection to those seeking refuge. According to a new UNHCR report, Ethiopia is now hosting more than 900,000 refugees in 2018. More than 99 per cent of them originate from four countries: South Sudan, Somalia, Eritrea and Sudan.

This map show all conflicts happened near Ethiopia from 2015 to 2018 based ACLED's data. There are 8226 battle conflicts in countries nearby Ethiopia. In contrast, Ethiopia only has 520 battle conflicts in last three years. Relative peaceful situation in Ethiopia is also one of the reasons that refugees and asylum-seekers move to Ethiopia.
